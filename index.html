Features

Learn about the latest advances in sustainabile technologies which are all incorparated in the Equinox Tower, allowing for the self production of all the elements!

Rain Water Captivity

1200 Gallons a Year

Planet Howler recives high levels of perception every 4 days consistently throughout the year, which we have grasped to create an efficient water solution.

Wind Energy

45km/s weekly speed

Various areas experience medium to high wind speeds which is the reason why we have windturbines.

Photovoltaic (PV) Panels

5% More Effective & Functional

Photocoltaic Panels are far superior to solar panels being 5% more efficient in enegry convertion while being the same cost and weight. The additional efficiency is crucial to maintaining a self-sustaining ecosystem on planet Howler

BREEAM Accreditation

Held by Equinox Tower

Equinox tower establishes the benchmark for the most sustainable building design, achieving the Platinum LEED and Outstanding BREEAM accreditation, which is the most rigorous standards for sustainable design.

Triple Glazed Windows

Design Feasibility

These windows allow for 70% of the day to pass without using any artificial light. Triple glazed windows help capture sunlight efficiently and make it so that no artificial lights are used for majority of the day.

3500 Data Points

Building Management System

This system will allow us to monitor the buildings effiency and sustainability which include viewing the water and energy consumption live.

Relevance

Team Equinox has not only identified but also addressed and came up with the ideal sustainable solution to the Earth's dystopian futuristic society.

Design Feasibility

Using the design concepts of platinum LEED building design and glued laminated timber, Equinox Tower is the most theoretically sound and feasible solution for the colonization of planet Howler.

Functionality

The technology that allows for the self-production of the elements imbeded in Equinox are the most unique sustainable advancements made to date. The creative techniques help fully sustain the residents and the tower itself, which are the self production of water, heat, power, and weather.

Innovation

We are able to convey and show the tangible proof that our tower provides, proving the innovation and functions that we are able to address. Not only does our tower function effectively in a completely new environment, but it is completely self-sustainable! This level of vision and execution successfully communicates the innovational aspect of our project.
